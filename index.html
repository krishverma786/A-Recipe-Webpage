Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestl√© MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to form a soft dough, avoiding over-kneading.
  3. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls.
  4. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
  5. Transfer the fried Gulab Jamuns into the cooled sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are added, bring the syrup to a boil again briefly, then remove from heat.
  6. Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamun warm, garnished with your favourite toppings.

Oreo Cake

Ingredients

  • Oreo Biscuit
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Crush oreo cookies and take oreo cookies in a mixer. Powder them.
  2. Now add in baking powder, sugar and milk. Puree them to a smooth mix.
  3. Now take a microwave safe bowl and grease it with oil generously.
  4. You can line the bowl with parchment paper and grease the paper, this way the cake will slide out easily.
  5. Pour the batter in the bowl and put it in the microwave on high for 5 mins.
  6. Once it is baked, remove it from oven and let it cool completely before inverting.
  7. If you used parchment paper you can invert it earlier.
  8. Once it is out, you can slice and serve.

Lemonade

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Combine 1 cup each of sugar and water and cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Set the syrup aside to cool for about 10 minutes.
  3. Combine the cooled syrup and freshly-squeezed lemon juice in a pitcher.
  4. Add the remaining water and stir, then pour over ice and enjoy!
